# Cryptocurrency Basics

New to cryptocurrency? This section will help you understand the fundamentals.

## What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is digital money that uses cryptography for security. Unlike traditional currency, it's:

- **Decentralized** - No central bank controls it
- **Secure** - Protected by advanced cryptography
- **Transparent** - All transactions are publicly recorded
- **Borderless** - Can be sent anywhere in the world instantly

## Common Questions

**"Is cryptocurrency safe?"**
Yes, when properly secured. Your seed phrase is the key - keep it safe!

**"Do donations in crypto affect our tax status?"**
Crypto donations are treated like other property donations. Consult your tax advisor.

**"What if the price changes?"**
Bitcoin fluctuates. USDC is pegged to the US Dollar for stability.
